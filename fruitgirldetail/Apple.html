Fruit
りんご
Apple

Date of discovery
2018/06/03

Location of discovery
中国 張家界
China Zhangjiajie

Details
張家界市の2,000以上の巨大石柱がある張家界国家森林公園に潜んでいた。りんごとは無縁と思える巨大石柱の天辺におり、探索困難であるなか奇跡的に発見された。高いところから遠くの景色を見渡すのが好きであったのではないかと推測されている。
It was hiding in Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, which has more than 2,000 giant stone pillars in Zhangjiajie City. It was miraculously found on the top of a giant stone pillar, which was not related to apples, and was difficult to search for. It is speculated that she may have liked to look out over the distant landscape from a high place.
